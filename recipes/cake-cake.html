Cake Flavoured Cake

Description

A unique metatwist on a the concept, and indeed the identity itself, of the everyday cake. Prepare for 🤯

Ingredients

  • Cake
  • Cake sticks
  • Caked cake
  • Cake icing
  • A pair of eyes and a mouth

Steps

  1. Sit on the floor in a quiet room with your legs crossed and eyes closed
  2. Focus on your breathing
  3. Allow your thoughts to wander towards cake, don't fight it
  4. Your creation will start to emerge from somewhere, don't rush the process
  5. Serve with cream

Spaghetti Flavoured Cake

Description

Lots of people are precious about their lasagna but when you boil it all down, it's really just spaghetti flavoured cake.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Veg
  • Sheets
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Get meat, veg, sheets and sauce
  2. Mix together and put it in the oven
  3. Cover in cheese
  4. Cook it for about 40 mins
  5. Take it out and when serving, say it is a Spaghetti Flavoured Cake
